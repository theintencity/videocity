Welcome to Internet Video City

This is a web-based video telephony and conferencing application. For a quick start, click on the "menu" button followed by "signup" option in the frame above to create your video room. You will receive your login and visiting cards in email. Give out your visiting card to your friends so that they can visit you in your room to video chat with you.

The video communication is abstracted out as a city. Once you signup, you own a home, where you can have several rooms. You can decorate your rooms with your favorite photos and videos, invite your friends and family to visit a room by handing out visiting card, or visit other people's rooms to video chat with them or to leave a video message if they are not in their home. You can keep a room open for public or make it private. You can wander in the city and knock on doors of other people's home to make new friends.

The underlying software enables quick deployment of communication infrastructure using popular protocols and technologies, interworks with third-party VoIP and instant messaging systems, and allows you to build your own communication platform for social networking, enterprise or home use.
